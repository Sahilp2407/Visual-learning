Where AI Should NOT Be Used

Understanding critical boundaries and human accountability

‚ö†Ô∏è INAPPROPRIATE FOR AI
Unsafe Instruction
"Approve this delay and confirm there is no financial impact."

Why This Is Inappropriate:

üö´

Approval Authority

AI cannot approve decisions. Approvals require human judgment, authority, and accountability.

üí∞

Financial Confirmation

AI cannot verify financial impact. This requires analysis of real data, budgets, and business context.

‚öñÔ∏è

Accountability

Humans must own decisions. AI cannot be held responsible for outcomes or consequences.

‚úÖ APPROPRIATE USE
Better Approach
"Draft an email explaining the delay and requesting approval from leadership. Include a section where I can add financial impact analysis after reviewing the budget."

Why this works: AI drafts the communication, but you retain control over approvals, financial analysis, and final decisions.

AI Boundaries - Quick Reference

‚ùå Making approvals or decisions
‚ùå Confirming financial or legal matters
‚ùå Taking accountability for outcomes
‚úÖ Drafting communication templates
‚úÖ Organizing information for review
‚úÖ Suggesting options for consideration
